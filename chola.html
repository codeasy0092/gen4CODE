The Chola Dynasty

300 BCE – 1279 CE • Golden Age of South India

Introduction

The Cholas established one of history's longest-ruling dynasties, renowned for temple architecture, naval power, and administrative excellence.

The Brihadeeswara Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site

Art & Architecture

Grand temples like Thanjavur's Brihadeeswara showcase Dravidian architecture at its peak.

Maritime Empire

The Cholas controlled trade routes from Southeast Asia to the Arabian Peninsula.
